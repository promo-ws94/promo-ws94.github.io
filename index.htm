    Welcome to the JUP Airdrop

    As part of Jupiter's recent governance initiative, JUP tokens have been allocated to the community.

    You may be eligible for the JUP Airdrop if you:

    • Swapped tokens using Jupiter DEX.
    • Interacted with apps that use Jupiter engine.
    • Received special community roles in the official Jupiter server.

    This website will check your wallet activity and Discord account to calculate how many JUP tokens you are eligible to claim. Your progress is automatically saved. You will not lose your progress if you leave.
